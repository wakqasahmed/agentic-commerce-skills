# Sources

Normative, externally verifiable guidance uses stable source IDs in square brackets. Recheck sources after material protocol changes and at least within the window below. Repository mechanics and evidence observed during an audit do not need citations.

Freshness window: 180 days.

## SRC-SCHEMA-PRODUCT

- Publisher: Schema.org
- Official URL: https://schema.org/Product
- Supported claim: Product markup supports product identifiers and product variant relationships, including SKU, GTIN, ProductGroup, and hasVariant properties.
- Specification version: 30.0
- Last verified: 2026-07-22
